My Biography

Born on October 28, 1955, in Seattle, Washington,

famed entrepreneur Bill Gates began to show an interest in computer programming at age 13.

Through technological innovation, keen business strategy and aggressive business tactics,

he and partner Paul Allen built the world's largest software business, Microsoft.

In the process, Gates became one of the richest men in the world.

In February 2014, Gates announced that he was stepping down as Microsoft's chairman.
